I’m a Machine Learning Engineer and researcher, completing a Master’s in Computer Science at [Georgia Tech](https://www.gatech.edu/). My research interests lie at the intersection of **representation learning** and **computer vision**. My current work focuses on learning robust representations for fine-grained tasks, including bridging the focal-to-soundscape domain gap in bioacoustics and multi-label plant classification, as well as exploring diffusion models.

I also co-lead [**DS@GT Applied Research & Competitions (ARC)**](https://dsgt-arc.org/), a student-run group advancing ML research through competitive challenges and peer-reviewed publications.
